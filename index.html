About the Artist

Born in Alma-Ata, Kazakhstan in 1979. I picked up a brush and never put it down. My work moves between the rigid beauty of geometric structures, the honest depth of realistic portraiture, and the uncontrolled energy of abstract expression.

Since 1990, based in Israel, I work across acrylics, charcoal, and mixed media — always chasing the next unexpected combination of form and feeling. Each piece is a conversation between control and chaos.
